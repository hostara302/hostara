Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  We stand behind our services with a straightforward refund policy.

✓ 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Our Promise

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all new hosting plans. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days of your first purchase, we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.

Eligible Refunds

You are eligible for a refund if:

  • You request a refund within 30 days of your initial service activation
  • This is your first hosting purchase with CandyHosting
  • Your account is in good standing (no ToS violations)

Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Renewal payments (only the first purchase is covered)
  • Accounts terminated for Terms of Service violations
  • Services cancelled after 30 days of activation
  • Any add-on services or domain registrations
  • Partial billing periods (we do not offer pro-rated refunds)

How to Request a Refund

1
Contact us via our contact page or email support@candyhosting.com within 30 days of activation.
2
Include your invoice number, registered email, and a brief reason for the refund.
3
Our team will review and confirm your eligibility within 1–2 business days.
4
Approved refunds are processed back via the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Service Credits

In cases of significant service disruptions that are our fault (e.g. extended downtime beyond our SLA), we may offer account credits as compensation. Credits are applied to your next invoice and have no cash value.

Cancellations

You may cancel your service at any time. Cancellation stops future billing but does not automatically trigger a refund. Please submit a refund request separately if you are within the 30-day window.

Upon cancellation, your hosting account and all associated data will be removed within 7 days.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact our support team. We're happy to help.
