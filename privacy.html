Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Your privacy matters to us at CandyHosting.

1. Information We Collect

When you use CandyHosting, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, phone number, and country
  • Billing information: Payment method details (processed securely, we do not store full card numbers)
  • Usage data: Server logs, IP addresses, bandwidth usage, and service activity
  • Support communications: Tickets, emails, and chat messages you send us

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provision and manage your hosting services
  • Send invoices, receipts, and service-related notifications
  • Respond to support requests
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Improve our services and platform

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Despite our best efforts, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your account credentials confidential.

4. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies to maintain your session and remember your preferences. We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party processors for payments (such as Lava.top and PayPal). These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them. We share only the minimum information necessary to process your payment.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. After account termination, we may retain certain data for up to 90 days for legal and billing purposes, after which it is securely deleted.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at our contact page.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify active customers of significant changes via email. Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, email us at privacy@candyhosting.com or use our contact page.
